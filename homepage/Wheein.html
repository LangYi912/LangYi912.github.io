Wheein

Wheein is the main vocalist and dancer in Mamamoo, she was born in Jeonju, Jeollabuk-do, South Korea on April 17, 1995, with the birth name Jung Whee In and graduated from Wonkwang Information Arts High School. Wheein considered herself the “Snack Queen'' because she loves eating snacks, and she also has a habit of drawing. The fact that she calls herself the “Snack Queen'' she loves to watch mukbang shows also known as the eating shows. Wheein has known Hwasa since middle school where they both shared two friendship tattoos. Wheein also has a really cute cat named Ggomo/Kkomo that appears in her daily photo/post.
