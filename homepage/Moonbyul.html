Moonbyul

Moonbyul is the main rapper and main dancer in Mamamoo, she was born in Bucheon, South Korea on December 22, 1992, with the birth name Moon Byul-Yi and attended Paekche Institute of the Arts. Moonbyul has two younger sisters, which means she is the oldest in her family. Moonbyul was originally auditioned to be a vocalist but changed to become a rapper instead. Moonbyul has a similar appearance to Xiumin from EXO so Eunhyuk from Super Junior considers her as “Xiumin in a wig,” she had the nickname “Black Hole” because she tends to sweat a lot. She was being credited for over 65 songs. Moonbyul isn’t good with roller coasters. Moonbyul, along with Hani (EXID), Jin (BTS), Ken (VIXX), and Sandeul (B1A4), has a group chat for 92-liners because they were born in 1992.
