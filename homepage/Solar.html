Solar

Solar is the leader and the main vocalist in Mamamoo, she was born in Gangseo-gu, Seoul, South Korea on February 21, 1991, with the birth name Kim Yong Sun and graduated from Modern K Music Academy University. Solar often considers herself the “mom” of Mamamoo because she was the oldest and the leader of the group so she has to take care of other members. Before Solar became a part of Mamamoo she wanted to be a Flight Attendant which was pretty interesting because Flight Attendant had nothing to do with Kpop. Solar was also the scaredy-cat in Mamamoo, that is because, in MBC Every1’s Showtime Season 7, Ep.4 it shows her fear of ghosts.
